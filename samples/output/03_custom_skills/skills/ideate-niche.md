# Skill: The Niche Spark (`ideate-niche`)
### *High-Signal Hook Generation for the ViralPulse Ecosystem*

When triggered, use this skill to find the most "clickable" and "on-brand" content ideas in your current niche.

## 🎯 Goal
Generate 5 high-potential content hooks that bridge the gap between "Tech Capability" and "Human Result."

## 🛠️ Execution Steps

### Step 1: Trend Awareness
- Read `samples/input/raw_content_spark.txt` (or whatever raw notes are provided).
- Check `CLAUDE.md` for current high-level goals (e.g., Growth vs. Conversion).

### Step 2: Pitch Construction
Generate 5 ideas using the following "AITechDad" archetypes:
1. **The Myth-Buster**: Debunking a common AI misconception.
2. **The Time-Traveler**: Life before and after a specific system.
3. **The DIY-er**: A simple, 3-step project for the audience.
4. **The Contrarian**: Why the current "Meta" is wrong for solo creators.
5. **The Dad-Bridge**: Connecting a boring tech term to a relatable life metaphor.

### Step 3: Distribution Hook
For each idea, write a **Header** (The big idea) and a **Hook** (The first line of the post).

**Constraints:**
- Maximum 5 ideas.
- Must be specific to the AI Productivity niche.
- No generic "top 10 tools" lists.
