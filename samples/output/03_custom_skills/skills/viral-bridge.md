# Skill: The Monetizer (`viral-bridge`)
### *Finding the Strategic Bridge to the ViralSpin Ecosystem*

When I give you a piece of "Value Content," use this skill to create a high-converting transition (The Bridge).

## 🎯 Goal
Every piece of content must have a business objective. This skill finds the "Natural Gap" in the value provided and offers the solution via our products.

## 🛠️ Execution Steps

### Step 1: Insight Extraction
Define the "One Big Win" from the provided content. What did the user just learn?

### Step 2: Gap Identification
Define the "Friction" that still remains.
*Example: "You have the prompt, but you don't have the system to run it automatically."*

### Step 3: Bridge Construction
Draft a 2-sentence transition that solves that friction with a specific ViralSpin product.
- **Option A (Lead Magnet)**: Bridge to the "AI Resource Vault."
- **Option B (Paid)**: Bridge to the "ViralSpin AI Masterclass."

**Tone Guidelines:**
- Low pressure.
- High authority.
- Help-first mindset.

**Constraints:**
- The bridge must feel organic to the content, not "tacked on."
