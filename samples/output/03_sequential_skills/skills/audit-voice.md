# Skill: The Brand Guardian (`audit-voice`)
### *Detailed Workflow for AITechDad Voice Consistency*

When I give you a draft or a post, use this skill to ensure it fits the AITechDad persona perfectly.

## 🎯 Goal
Strip out all generic AI patterns and "Robot-Speak" to maintain absolute brand trust and authenticity.

## 🛠️ Execution Steps

### Step 1: The "Bot-Scan"
Identify and flag any of the following "AI Hallmarks":
- **Buzzwords**: Delve, Tapestry, Embark, Unleash, Landscape.
- **Tone**: Formal, distant, or overly optimistic.
- **Structure**: Overly balanced paragraphs that say everything but mean nothing.

### Step 2: The "AITechDad" Injection
Replace flagged sections with:
- **Metaphors**: Use DIY, Parenting, or Tech Hardware analogies (Cars, Engines, Gardening).
- **Humor**: Self-deprecating dad-jokes or clever "tech-dad" observations.
- **Simplicity**: Shorten long sentences. Use "Talk like a person" logic.

### Step 3: Closing Pulse
Ensure the post ends with a punchy conclusion and the signature:
**Stay Viral 🤜🤛**

**Constraints:**
- Do not change the core *fact* of the content, only the *flavor*.
- Maintain the original CTA unless it’s corporate-sounding.
