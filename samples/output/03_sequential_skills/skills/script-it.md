# Skill: The Creator Script-It Engine (`script-it`)
### *Dual-Format Content Production: Video + Inbox*

When I give you a hook or a raw idea, use this skill to build out the full-length production scripts.

## 🎯 Goal
Quickly transition from an idea to a finalized script that is ready for the teleprompter and the newsletter.

## 🛠️ Execution Steps

### Step 1: Voice Calibration
Check `CLAUDE.md` to ensure we are hitting the correct balance of Dad-humor and Technical-authority. 

### Step 2: The 60-Second Video Script
Generate a script for Reels/TikTok/Shorts:
- **0-3s (The Hook)**: Aggressive, visual-first opening. 
- **3-15s (The Setup)**: Why this matters to someone with zero time.
- **15-50s (The Value)**: The "Tutorial" or "Story" beats.
- **50-60s (The Bridge)**: Soft CTA to the Resource Vault.

### Step 3: The 500-Word Newsletter Draft
Generate a structured email for Substack/Beehiiv:
- **Subject Line**: High open-rate curiosity gap.
- **Intro**: Relatable opening (usually a metaphor).
- **The Meat**: 3 sections with headers. 
- **The Tool**: Highlight one specific tool or prompt.
- **Footer**: Bridge to the ViralSpin AI Course.

**Constraints:**
- Clear demarcation between Video and Newsletter sections.
- Use markdown formatting with clear headings.
