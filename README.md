# Claude Code for Solo Creators 🤜🤛
### *The "Zero-to-Hero" Agentic Workflow*

Welcome to the future of content production. You aren't here to learn how to "chat" with AI—you're here to build an **Engine.**

This course will take you from a raw, messy idea to a fully automated, agentic content factory in 6 modules.

---

## 🏗️ The Curriculum

### 🚀 **[Module 1: The Launchpad](course/01_installation.md)**
Get the engine running in under 5 minutes. We'll set up your **Claude/Antigravity Desktop App** or the **CLI Engine** and build your "Creator Brain" (`CLAUDE.md`) so the AI knows exactly who you are.

### ✨ **[Module 2: The Magic Moment](course/02_first_magic.md)**
Experience the raw power of context. We'll turn a messy transcript or brain dump into a focused strategy using the discovery engine—no copy-pasting required.

### 🏭 **[Module 3: The Content Factory (The Big Five)](course/03_sequential_skills.md)**
This is where we build your production line. You'll create and run the **Big Five Skills**:
1. `ideate-niche`: Finding the high-signal hooks.
2. `script-it`: Turning hooks into full scripts.
3. `multiply`: One script into 5+ platform-specific assets.
4. `audit-voice`: Stripping out "AI-isms" to restore your unique brand.
5. `viral-bridge`: Connecting your content to your ecosystem.

### 💎 **[Module 4: Website Vibe-Building](course/04_website_vibe_building.md)**
Let's build something beautiful. We'll use **Vibe-Coding** to architect a premium, glassmorphic brand home (`index.html`). Learn how to iterate on design like a Creative Director.

### 🛰️ **[Module 5: The Agentic Pulse](course/05_agentic_automation.md)**
Spawn an army of parallel agents. Learn how to scout Reddit, X, and the news simultaneously for trends, and run "Competitor Audits" at 10x speed.

### 🔁 **[Module 6: The Infinite Content Loop](course/06_infinite_scale.md)**
Scale to infinity. Wrap everything into a repeatable system and see the path to advanced visual manifestation—talking heads, viral carousels, and reverse-engineered viral logic.
